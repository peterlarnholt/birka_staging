Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time. You will continue to have access to your plan's features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the free tier.

What payment methods do you accept?

Payments are processed by Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Paddle supports credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and various local payment methods depending on your region.

Is there a free trial?

The free tier is available forever with no credit card required. Simply open WhatsApp and start chatting with dabbl.ai. When you're ready for more messages and features, you can upgrade at any time.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. EU consumers benefit from a 14-day cooling-off period. We also offer refunds at our discretion for any reasonable request. See our Refund Policy for full details.

Do I need to create an account?

No. dabbl.ai works entirely through WhatsApp. There is no separate app to download and no account to create. Just message us on WhatsApp and you're ready to go.

What happens if I hit my daily message limit?

If you reach your daily message limit, dabbl will let you know and you can continue the next day. Your limit resets every day at midnight (Lisbon time). You can upgrade your plan at any time for more messages.

Will I be charged VAT?

Paddle handles all tax obligations as our Merchant of Record. The price displayed may be subject to VAT depending on your location. Paddle will show you the final amount including any applicable taxes before you complete your purchase.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the pro-rated difference immediately. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing period.

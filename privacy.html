Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how EXCOM AI (operating as Handled / dabbl.ai), referred to as "we", "us", or "our", collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use the dabbl.ai service ("Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK Data Protection Act 2018, and other applicable data protection laws.

2. Data Controller

EXCOM AI is the data controller for the personal data processed through the dabbl.ai service. You can contact us regarding data protection matters at:

Email: hello@handled.sh
Website: dabbl.ai

3. What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide and improve the Service:

Data Type Details Purpose
Phone number Your WhatsApp phone number Account identification, service delivery
Messages Messages you send to dabbl.ai via WhatsApp Processing your requests, generating AI responses
Usage data Message count, feature usage, timestamps Service management (e.g., enforcing message limits), improving the Service
Subscription data Subscription tier, billing status Managing your subscription and access level
Preferences Language preference, briefing settings, reminders Personalising your experience

3.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or physical address (unless you voluntarily provide it in a message)
  • We do not use cookies — the Service operates entirely through WhatsApp
  • We do not collect location data unless you explicitly share it in a message
  • We do not access your WhatsApp contacts, photos, or other phone data

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under the GDPR:

  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)) — Processing is necessary to provide the Service you have requested
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — For improving the Service, preventing abuse, and ensuring security
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) — Where you have given explicit consent, such as opting in to daily briefings
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) — Where required by applicable law

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and deliver the Service, including generating AI responses to your messages
  • Manage your subscription and enforce usage limits
  • Send you daily briefings, reminders, and other features you have enabled
  • Improve and develop the Service
  • Prevent abuse and ensure the security of the Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. Third Parties and Data Sharing

We share your data with the following third parties only as necessary to provide the Service:

6.1 WhatsApp / Meta

The Service operates through the WhatsApp Business API. Your messages are transmitted via WhatsApp's infrastructure. WhatsApp/Meta's privacy policy applies to the transmission and storage of messages on their platform. We use the official WhatsApp Business API and comply with Meta's data handling requirements.

6.2 Paddle (Payment Processor)

Paddle.com Market Limited acts as our Merchant of Record for all paid subscriptions. When you subscribe to a paid plan, Paddle collects and processes your payment information directly. We do not have access to your full credit card details or payment credentials. Paddle's privacy policy governs their handling of your payment data.

6.3 AI Model Providers

We use third-party AI models to generate responses to your messages. Your message content is sent to these AI providers for processing. We select providers that offer appropriate data protection safeguards. Message content sent to AI providers is not used to train their models where such opt-out mechanisms are available.

6.4 Infrastructure Providers

We use cloud hosting providers to run the Service. These providers process data on our behalf under data processing agreements that comply with GDPR requirements.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK. Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Other legally recognised transfer mechanisms

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfil the purposes described in this policy:

  • Message content: Retained for up to 90 days to provide conversation context, then automatically deleted
  • Usage data: Retained for up to 12 months for analytics and service improvement, then anonymised or deleted
  • Account data (phone number, preferences): Retained for as long as you use the Service. Deleted within 30 days of account closure or upon request
  • Subscription data: Retained for as long as required by applicable tax and accounting laws (typically 7 years for financial records)

9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to data portability — You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to restrict processing — You can request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to object — You can object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@handled.sh. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Portugal, the relevant authority is the Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados (CNPD). In the UK, it is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

10. Cookies and Tracking

The dabbl.ai service operates entirely through WhatsApp. We do not use cookies, web beacons, or similar tracking technologies on the Service itself.

Our website (dabbl.ai) may use minimal, essential cookies for basic functionality. No third-party tracking cookies are used on our website.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete that data promptly.

12. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption in transit, access controls, and regular security reviews.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the Service (WhatsApp message) or by updating this page. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data protection rights, please contact us at:

Email: hello@handled.sh
Website: dabbl.ai

Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all paid subscriptions for the dabbl.ai service ("Service") provided by EXCOM AI (operating as Handled / dabbl.ai). All payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited ("Paddle"), which acts as our Merchant of Record.

We want you to be satisfied with dabbl.ai. If you are not, we are committed to making things right.

2. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your dabbl.ai subscription at any time. Here is what happens when you cancel:

  • Your cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You will continue to have full access to your paid plan's features until then.
  • After your billing period ends, your account will automatically revert to the free tier (5 messages per day, basic AI model).
  • No further charges will be made to your payment method.
  • You can re-subscribe at any time if you change your mind.

How to cancel

You can cancel your subscription by:

  • Sending a message to dabbl.ai on WhatsApp saying "cancel subscription"
  • Emailing us at hello@handled.sh
  • Using the subscription management link in your Paddle receipt email

3. EU Consumer Right of Withdrawal (Cooling-Off Period)

If you are a consumer in the European Union, you have the right to withdraw from your subscription within 14 days of your initial purchase, without giving any reason, in accordance with EU Directive 2011/83/EU.

During this 14-day cooling-off period:

  • You may request a full refund of your subscription payment.
  • This right applies to your first subscription payment only.
  • To exercise this right, contact us at hello@handled.sh with your request.

Please note that if you have actively used the Service during the cooling-off period, we may deduct an amount proportional to the services provided up to the point of withdrawal.

4. Discretionary Refunds

Outside the EU cooling-off period, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis at our discretion. We may offer refunds in situations including, but not limited to:

  • Technical issues that prevented you from using the Service for a significant portion of your billing period
  • Being charged after you attempted to cancel your subscription
  • Duplicate or erroneous charges
  • Other circumstances where a refund is fair and reasonable

We may offer a full refund, a pro-rata refund based on the unused portion of your billing period, or account credit, depending on the circumstances.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us using one of the following methods:

Contact us for a refund

Email: hello@handled.sh

WhatsApp: Send a message to dabbl.ai saying "I'd like a refund"

Paddle: You can also contact Paddle directly using the link in your receipt email

When requesting a refund, please include:

  • The phone number associated with your dabbl.ai account
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Your Paddle receipt or transaction reference (if available)

6. Processing Time

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days. Once a refund is approved:

  • Refunds are processed by Paddle and typically take 5–10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your payment method and financial institution.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • You will receive a confirmation email from Paddle once the refund has been processed.

7. Free Tier

The dabbl.ai free tier is available at no cost and does not involve any payment. As no charge is made, refunds do not apply to the free tier.

8. Chargebacks

If you have a concern about a charge, we encourage you to contact us first before initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider. We are committed to resolving any billing issues quickly and fairly. Initiating a chargeback without contacting us may result in the suspension of your account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated to you via the Service or email.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at:

Email: hello@handled.sh
Website: dabbl.ai
